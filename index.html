Privacy Policy for HalalScan – Halal Food Identifier

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

HalalScan ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application, HalalScan – Halal Food Identifier ("the App"), handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Required: We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email, or any personal details.
  • Camera Access: The App uses your device’s camera solely to scan product barcodes. Images or video from your camera are not stored or transmitted.
  • Internet Access (Optional): The App may connect to public databases (such as Open Food Facts) to retrieve product information. Requests are made without collecting personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

The App only processes the barcode you scan and the product data retrieved from local data or public APIs. This information is used solely to display classification results (Halal, Haram, Doubtful) and the ingredient breakdown.

3. Data Storage

The App does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Product data may be cached locally on your device for faster access. This stays only on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use services such as the Open Food Facts API. These services have their own privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. It is safe for all ages because no personal information is collected.

6. Your Choices

  • Camera use is optional.
  • Manual barcode entry is available.
  • Offline mode is limited but still useful for accessing the “Learn” content and product history stored locally.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted here with a new Effective Date.

8. Contact Us

Thank you for using HalalScan.
