Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 22, 2025

Welcome to HalalScan (“we”, “our”, “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application HalalScan – Food Scanner handles and protects your information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Required: We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location.
  • Camera Access: The app uses your device’s camera only to scan product barcodes. No photos or videos are stored or transmitted.
  • Internet Access: The app may connect to online sources to retrieve product information but does not collect or share any personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

The app processes only barcode data to identify products and display halal classification results (Halal, Haram, or Doubtful). We do not track or profile users in any way.

3. Advertising (AdMob)

HalalScan displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and unique device identifiers to show relevant ads. Learn more about how Google uses data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

You can manage or opt out of personalized ads by visiting: https://www.google.com/settings/ads.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and ensure that no personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or transmitted.

5. Children’s Privacy

HalalScan can be safely used by all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Health Information Disclaimer

HalalScan provides informational guidance about halal and haram food products. It does not offer medical, dietary, or nutritional advice and should not be used to diagnose or treat health conditions.

7. Links to Other Sites

Our app may include links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.
