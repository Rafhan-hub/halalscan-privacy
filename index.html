HalalScan – Halal Food Identifier

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 13, 2025

HalalScan (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application, HalalScan – Halal Food Identifier (“the App”), handles and protects your information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details such as name or email.
  • Camera Access: The app uses your device’s camera only to scan product barcodes. Images or video from your camera are not stored or transmitted.
  • Internet Access: The app may connect to public databases (like Open Food Facts) to retrieve product details. These requests are made without collecting personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

The app processes only the barcode you scan and the related product data from local sources or public APIs. This data is used solely to show halal classification results (Halal, Haram, or Doubtful) and ingredient breakdowns.

3. Data Storage

The app does not collect, store, or share personal information. Product data may be temporarily cached on your device for faster performance — this data remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party sources or APIs such as Open Food Facts. These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review separately.

5. Health Information Disclaimer

HalalScan provides religious and informational guidance by identifying halal or haram food products. It does not provide medical, nutritional, or dietary advice. The app is not intended to diagnose, treat, or monitor any health condition.

6. Advertising and Analytics

HalalScan displays ads provided by third-party networks such as Google AdMob. These services may use cookies and unique identifiers to deliver personalized ads. Learn more about Google’s data use at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We may also use analytics tools (such as Firebase Analytics) to understand app usage and improve user experience. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

7. Children’s Privacy

HalalScan is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from children. The app can be used safely without providing personal information.

8. Your Choices

  • Camera use is optional; manual barcode entry is available.
  • You can control ad personalization in your device settings or Google’s Ads Settings page.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our app or policies. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date.
