Halal Finder – Food Scanner

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 27, 2025

Welcome to Halal Finder – Food Scanner (“we”, “our”, “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • No Account Required: We do not require users to create an account or submit personal details such as name, email address, or phone number.
  • Camera Access: Used solely for scanning product barcodes. No images, photos, or videos are stored or transmitted.
  • Internet Access: Used to retrieve product information and halal status from online databases. These requests do not include personal identifiers.
  • Advertising Data: Third-party advertising SDKs (such as Google AdMob) may collect:
    • Advertising ID (AAID / IDFA)
    • IP address
    • Device type, OS version
    • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
    This data is used for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and performance measurement.
  • Analytics & Diagnostics: Anonymous usage data such as app version, device model, and crash logs may be collected to improve stability and performance.

2. How We Use Information

Barcode data is processed locally on your device to determine halal classification (Halal, Haram, or Doubtful). We do not sell or trade personal data.

3. Advertising & Analytics

We use Google AdMob to display ads and may use analytics services such as Firebase.

  • How Google uses data: Google Privacy Policy
  • Partner data usage: Google Partner-Sites Policy
  • Ad personalization controls: Google Ads Settings

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland are shown a consent dialog via Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to choose personalized or non-personalized ads.

4. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR (EU/UK) and CCPA (California), regarding data processed by third-party services integrated into the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly.

6. Health & Religious Disclaimer

Halal Finder provides informational guidance only and is not affiliated with any official halal certification authority. Always verify with official sources.

7. Third-Party Links

The app may include links or ads from third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Your Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings

  • Uninstall the app to stop all processing
